Chemical Carbon Conversion

"

Chemical carbon conversion is a subset of Direct Air Carbon Capture with the explicit goal of using carbon dioxide captured from the atmosphere for a set of chemical processes to convert the carbon into other materials. In the case of Lanza Technologies, the output are other petrochemical products.
